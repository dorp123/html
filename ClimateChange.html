People Climate March

Stop all emmision within a decade!

More than 20,000 people, many of them students skipping school, joined Swedish teen activist Greta Thunberg to protest against climate change in the German capital and all over the world.
Carrying creative signs with slogans like: ‘There is no Planet B’, or Harry Potter-themed ‘Even Voldemort knows this is wrong'. With loud music and chants, there was a party atmosphere in the air. But it was more than that: there was a serious message behind it.
